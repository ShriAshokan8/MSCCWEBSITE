Subject Categories

Maths

Challenge yourself with complex mathematical problems, from pure mathematics to applied problem-solving. Topics include:

  • Algebra and number theory
  • Geometry and trigonometry
  • Calculus and advanced mathematics
  • Mathematical modeling and statistics

Science

Explore the natural world through hands-on experiments and theoretical challenges. Areas covered:

  • Physics: Mechanics, thermodynamics, and modern physics
  • Chemistry: Organic, inorganic, and physical chemistry
  • Biology: Cell biology, genetics, and ecology

Computing

Develop programming skills and computational thinking through practical challenges:

  • Algorithm design
  • Programming
  • Web development and app creation
  • Artificial intelligence

Combined/STEM

Interdisciplinary challenges that combine all three subjects:

  • Mathematical modeling of scientific phenomena
  • Computational solutions to real-world problems
  • Data analysis and visualization projects
  • Innovation challenges and design thinking

How to Participate

Your school/tutor group will be spoken to or emailed by the MSC Team.
