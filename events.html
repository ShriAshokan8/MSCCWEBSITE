2025 Test Run

Our flagship event for 2025 combines the excitement of competition with the joy of collaborative learning.

Building on our successful launch in September 2024, the 2025 test run will feature expanded activities, more engaging challenges, and greater opportunities for students to showcase their STEM skills across Mathematics, Science, and Computing.

This comprehensive event spans multiple weeks during STEM Week and includes both individual achievements and team collaboration, ensuring every participant can contribute and excel.
