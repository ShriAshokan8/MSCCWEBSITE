Events & Competitions

Join us for exciting STEM events, competitions, and learning opportunities throughout the 2024-25 academic year.

2025 Test Run Schedule

  • March 7, 2025: STEM Week Launch - Competition begins
  • June 13, 2025: Awards ceremony and celebration

Subject Categories

Mathematics

Challenge yourself with complex mathematical problems, from pure mathematics to applied problem-solving. Topics include:

  • Algebra and number theory
  • Geometry and trigonometry
  • Calculus and advanced mathematics
  • Mathematical modeling and statistics

Science

Explore the natural world through hands-on experiments and theoretical challenges. Areas covered:

  • Physics: Mechanics, thermodynamics, and modern physics
  • Chemistry: Organic, inorganic, and physical chemistry
  • Biology: Cell biology, genetics, and ecology
  • Earth Sciences: Environmental science and geology

Computing

Develop programming skills and computational thinking through practical challenges:

  • Algorithm design and data structures
  • Programming in Python, Java, and C++
  • Web development and app creation
  • Artificial intelligence and machine learning basics

Combined STEM

Interdisciplinary challenges that combine all three subjects:

  • Mathematical modeling of scientific phenomena
  • Computational solutions to real-world problems
  • Data analysis and visualization projects
  • Innovation challenges and design thinking

Upcoming Events

  • Weekly workshops: Subject-specific skill-building sessions
  • Guest speakers: Industry professionals and university researchers
  • Peer tutoring: Advanced students supporting newcomers
  • Project showcases: Present your innovations to the community

How to Participate

Registration for the 2025 test run is now open! Contact any MSC team member or visit the main office to sign up. Whether you're a beginner or advanced student, there's a place for you in our community.
