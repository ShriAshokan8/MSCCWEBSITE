About the Competition

The MSC Competition is an exciting five-day event crafted to engage young minds in Mathematics, Science, and Computing through a series of quizzes and hands-on projects. Targeting Year 5 to Year 7 students, this competition offers a unique opportunity to showcase skills across these core subjects in a fun, collaborative environment. Each of the first three days focuses on one subject—Maths, Science, or Computing—allowing participants to immerse themselves in activities tailored to inspire curiosity and highlight their strengths in each area. The fourth day brings an interdisciplinary approach, combining all three subjects to encourage students to think across fields and apply their knowledge creatively.

The fifth day is a thrilling finale that includes the much-anticipated awards ceremony, where students present their projects, share their experiences, and celebrate each other's accomplishments. Throughout the competition, students engage in stimulating quizzes that test their knowledge and quick thinking, while the projects encourage teamwork, critical thinking, and practical application of STEM concepts. These projects challenge participants to explore real-world problems, encouraging innovation and solution-driven thinking.

The MSC Competition isn’t just about testing knowledge; it’s about fostering a love for learning, building confidence, and creating a sense of community. Each participant is given the chance to shine in a supportive environment, leaving the event with enhanced skills, new friendships, and a deeper appreciation for STEM subjects. With quizzes that keep them on their toes and projects that encourage exploration, students walk away with memorable experiences and a sense of pride in their achievements.
