Our Mission

The MSC Competition is dedicated to encouraging a passion for STEM subjects among students. We bring together young minds to explore and excel in Mathematics, Science, and Computing through friendly competition.

Who Can Participate?

Open to all Year 5 to Year 7 students across participating schools. Join us to represent your school and showcase your knowledge and skills! You will be in groups of 4!
