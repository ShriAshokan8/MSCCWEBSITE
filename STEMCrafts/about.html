About STEM Crafts Club

What We Do

STEM Crafts Club is a fun, creative after-school activity where students design and make handcrafted items using simple, affordable materials. We turn creative ideas into real products that people love to buy!

Our Purpose

We believe in learning by doing, and giving back. Every item we create serves a double purpose:

  • Skill Building: Students learn crafting techniques, creativity, and basic business skills
  • Fundraising: Sales revenue buys more materials and supports charitable causes

How It Works

  1. Create: Students design and craft items during club sessions
  2. Prepare: Items are quality-checked and packaged attractively
  3. Sell: Products are sold to teachers and students
  4. Reinvest: Money goes back into materials
  5. Give Back: Extra funds support chosen charities

What Makes Us Special

  • Low-cost materials: We use affordable, easy-to-find supplies
  • Student-led: Members drive the creative process
  • Purposeful: Every sale supports future projects and charity
  • Fun: It's all about creativity, community, and making a difference

Join Us

Whether you love crafting, want to try something new, or just want to be part of a club that gives back, STEM Crafts Club is the place for you!
