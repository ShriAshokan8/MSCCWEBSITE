Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 November 2025

STEM Crafts Club is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal data in accordance with UK data protection laws, including the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Who We Are (Data Controller)

Data Controller:
Contact Email: stemcrafts@mscinitiative.app

We are responsible for deciding how we hold and use personal information about you. If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your personal data, please contact us using the email address above.

2. What Personal Data We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

When you contact us or interact with our services, we may collect:

  • Contact Information: Your name and email address when you send us a message via our contact page
  • Communication Content: The content of any messages, questions, or feedback you send to us
  • Purchase Information: If you purchase items from us in person, we may collect your name and contact details for order fulfilment (no online payment processing currently)

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain technical information:

  • Device and Browser Information: Your device type, browser type and version, operating system
  • Usage Data: Pages you visit, time spent on pages, navigation paths
  • Technical Data: IP address, time zone settings, and general location data (country/region level)
  • Server Logs: Our web hosting provider may maintain standard server logs for security and performance monitoring

3. How We Use Your Personal Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

Contact and Communication

  • To respond to your enquiries and messages
  • To provide information about our club activities and handmade items
  • To communicate about orders or purchases (if applicable)

Legal Basis: Consent (when you contact us) and Legitimate Interests (to respond to your enquiries and operate our club effectively)

Website Operation and Improvement

  • To operate and maintain our website
  • To improve website performance, user experience, and content
  • To ensure website security and prevent fraud or misuse

Legal Basis: Legitimate Interests (to provide a secure, functional, and improved website service)

Order Fulfilment (if applicable)

  • To process and fulfil purchases of our handmade items
  • To manage inventory and plan future projects

Legal Basis: Contract (to fulfil our obligations when you purchase items from us)

4. Legal Bases for Processing

Under UK GDPR, we must have a legal basis for processing your personal data. We rely on the following:

  • Consent: You have given clear permission for us to process your personal data for a specific purpose (e.g., contacting us, receiving marketing updates if you opt in)
  • Contract: Processing is necessary to fulfil a contract with you (e.g., when you purchase items from us)
  • Legitimate Interests: Processing is necessary for our legitimate interests (e.g., improving our website, responding to enquiries, preventing fraud) and does not override your rights and freedoms

5. Who We Share Your Data With

We may share your personal data with the following third parties, but only when necessary:

Service Providers

  • Web Hosting: Our website is hosted by a third-party hosting provider who may have access to server logs and technical data
  • Email Services: If you contact us by email, your message is processed through standard email infrastructure
  • Form Processing: Contact forms may be processed through secure third-party services (e.g., Formspree or similar)

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties. All service providers are required to handle your data securely and only for the purposes we specify.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your personal data if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights, safety, or the safety of others.

6. Where Your Data Is Stored

Your personal data is primarily stored:

  • Within the UK and EEA: Our hosting and most services are based in the UK or European Economic Area
  • International Transfers: Some service providers (e.g., email infrastructure, CDNs) may process data internationally. Where this occurs, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as standard contractual clauses or adequacy decisions

7. How Long We Keep Your Data

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy:

  • Contact Enquiries: Typically retained for up to 2 years after your last contact, or as long as needed to resolve your enquiry
  • Purchase Records: Retained for accounting and warranty purposes, typically up to 6-7 years in line with legal requirements
  • Server Logs: Automatically deleted or anonymised within 90 days (managed by our hosting provider)
  • Marketing Consent: Retained until you withdraw consent or we no longer need to contact you

After these periods, we will securely delete or anonymise your data so you can no longer be identified.

8. Your Rights Under UK GDPR

Under UK data protection law, you have the following rights:

  • Right of Access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: You can ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure ('Right to be Forgotten'): You can request that we delete your personal data in certain circumstances
  • Right to Restriction of Processing: You can ask us to restrict how we use your data in certain situations
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request a copy of your data in a commonly used, machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: You can object to processing based on legitimate interests or direct marketing
  • Rights Related to Automated Decision-Making: We do not use automated decision-making or profiling

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at stemcrafts@mscinitiative.app. We will respond to your request within one month.

9. Right to Complain

If you are unhappy with how we handle your personal data, you have the right to complain to the UK's data protection supervisory authority:

Information Commissioner's Office (ICO)
Website: https://ico.org.uk/
Telephone: 0303 123 1113

We encourage you to contact us first so we can try to resolve any concerns.

10. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Essential Cookies Only

Our website currently uses only essential technical cookies and browser storage that are strictly necessary for the website to function properly. These include:

  • Service Worker Cache: Stores static website assets (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, images) locally in your browser to enable offline access and faster loading. This does not store personal data.
  • Session Data: Temporary data needed for navigation and basic website functionality

No Tracking or Analytics: We do not currently use analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) or third-party tracking cookies that collect personal data about your browsing habits.

Third-Party Services

Our website loads fonts from Google Fonts. This may involve a connection to Google's servers and is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. No personal data is intentionally shared with Google.

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality. For information on managing cookies in common browsers:

  • Chrome
  • Firefox
  • Safari
  • Edge

11. Children's Privacy

STEM Crafts Club is a school-based club primarily involving students. We take children's privacy seriously:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 through our website without parental consent
  • Contact via our website is intended for parents, guardians, teachers, or students with appropriate permission
  • Any in-person club activities involving students under 18 are managed through our school with appropriate safeguarding measures and parental consent

If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child without proper consent, please contact us immediately.

12. Data Security

We take appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse:

  • Secure Transmission: Our website uses HTTPS encryption to protect data transmitted between your browser and our server
  • Access Controls: Personal data is accessible only to authorised club organisers and administrators
  • Secure Storage: Data is stored securely with reputable hosting and email providers
  • Regular Reviews: We regularly review our security practices and update them as needed

While we strive to protect your personal data, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but will notify you of any data breaches as required by law.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or website functionality. When we make significant changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of our website after changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

14. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this privacy policy or how we handle your personal data, please contact us:

STEM Crafts Club
Email: stemcrafts@mscinitiative.app
Website: https://mscinitiative.app/STEMCrafts

We will do our best to respond to your enquiry within a reasonable timeframe, typically within one month.

Thank you for taking the time to understand how we protect your privacy. Your trust is important to us, and we're committed to handling your data responsibly and transparently.
