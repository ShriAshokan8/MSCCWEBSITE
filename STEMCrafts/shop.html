STEM Crafts Club Shop

Welcome to our shop! All items are lovingly handmade by STEM Crafts Club members using affordable craft materials.

Why Buy From Us?

  • Handmade with Care: Every item is unique and crafted by students
  • Support Learning: Your purchase helps us buy more materials
  • Give Back: Extra funds go to charitable causes
  • Affordable Prices: Quality handmade items at reasonable prices

Available Items

We regularly create new items for sale, including:

  • Greeting cards
  • Bookmarks
  • Keychains
  • Magnets
  • Gift tags
  • Small decorations
  • And more!

How to Purchase

Items are currently sold directly at school to teachers and students. If you're interested in purchasing our handmade crafts, please contact us for more information about availability and pricing.

Coming Soon: We're working on setting up an online ordering system to make it even easier to support our club!
