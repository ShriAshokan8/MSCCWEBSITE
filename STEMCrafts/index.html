Welcome to STEM Crafts Club

Explore, Create, Make a Difference

STEM Crafts Club is where curiosity meets creativity! We're a fun after-school club where students experiment with DIY projects, explore hands-on making, and discover how science, technology, engineering, and maths come together in real, tangible ways.

Using simple, low-cost craft materials, we turn ideas into reality. Then, we share what we've made by selling our creations to support future experiments and give back to charity.
