Welcome to STEM Crafts Club

What It Is

A fun after-school club where students use DIY, low-cost craft materials to make creative projects inspired by STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Maths).

These projects are then sold to teachers and sometimes students, and the money earned is used to:

  • Buy more materials for future sessions
  • Donate to charity

It's simple, hands-on, creative, and has a positive purpose.

⭐ What We Make

Everything is made using cheap, easy-to-find materials like:

  • Paper and cardboard
  • Beads and buttons
  • Fabric scraps
  • Recycled materials
  • Paint and markers

⭐ How the Selling Works

  1. Items are made in the club
  2. Quality checked
  3. Packaged nicely (labels, tags, simple wrapping)
  4. Sold to teachers or students
  5. Money collected
  6. Used to buy more craft materials
  7. Anything extra goes to a chosen charity
