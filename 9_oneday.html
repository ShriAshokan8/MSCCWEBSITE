1-Day MSC Competition Plan (9:30 AM to 4:30 PM)

9:30 AM - 9:45 AM: Opening Ceremony (15 minutes)

A short welcome speech to explain the competition, introduce the judges, and go over the rules.

9:45 AM - 11:30 AM: Quiz Time (1 hour 45 minutes)

How it works:
All 20 teams answer Maths, Science, and Computing questions together in the first part (1 hour).
The top 10 teams then do a quick quiz round (30 minutes) for extra points.

11:30 AM - 12:30 PM: Lunch Break (1 hour)

Everyone eats, chats, and gets ready for the big project presentations.

12:30 PM - 3:15 PM: Project Presentations (2 hours 45 minutes)

How it works:
All 20 teams will show their projects to all the judges and other teams.
Each team gets 6 minutes: 4 minutes to explain their project and 2 minutes to answer questions.
After every 7 teams, we’ll have a 5-minute break to reset and keep on track.

3:15 PM - 3:30 PM: Short Break (15 minutes)

A quick break to relax before the finale.

3:30 PM - 4:30 PM: Awards and Finale (1 hour)

What happens:
Announce the quiz winners (Top 3 teams).
Announce the best projects (Best Overall, Runner-Up, Most Creative).
Give out trophies, medals, and certificates.
Finish with a thank-you speech.

How This Will Work

Quiz:

Teams answer questions at the same time in the first part. The second part is for the top teams.
Answers are scored quickly so we stay on time.

Project Presentations:

Every team gets the same amount of time (6 minutes).
A timer will help teams know when their time is up.

Judges:

All the judges will watch every project and write their scores right after each one.

What’s Important

  • Keep track of time! Everyone has to follow the schedule so we can finish on time.
  • Be prepared! Teams should practice their presentations to fit within 6 minutes.
