Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about MSC Initiative? Find quick answers to common questions below, or contact us for more information.

What is the MSC Initiative?

The MSC Initiative is a student-led STEM movement that brings together enthusiastic students interested in Mathematics, Science, and Computing. We organize competitions, events, and activities to inspire innovation and excellence in STEM education.

When was MSC Initiative founded?

MSC Initiative was officially launched on 23rd September 2024, starting as a competition and growing into a comprehensive STEM community.

How can I join MSC Initiative?

You can join by participating in our events and competitions. Visit our Events page for upcoming opportunities, or contact us to learn more about getting involved.

When is the first MSC Competition?

Our first MSC Competition is scheduled to begin on 7th March 2025 during STEM Week. Stay updated by checking our calendar and newsletters.

Is there a cost to participate?

Our 2024-25 test run is free for all participants. We believe in making STEM education accessible to everyone.

What subjects does MSC Initiative cover?

We focus on three main areas: Mathematics, Science, and Computing. Our activities include problem-solving challenges, experiments, coding projects, and interdisciplinary STEM activities.

How can I stay updated on MSC activities?

Subscribe to our newsletters, check our calendar regularly, and follow our announcements. You can also contact us to receive direct updates.

Who can participate in MSC Initiative?

MSC Initiative welcomes students from all backgrounds and skill levels. Whether you're a beginner or advanced in STEM subjects, there's a place for you in our community.
