Tournament Structure:

  • Semi-Finals: 20 schools in 10 one-on-one matchups, 10 winners advance
  • Grand Final 1: 10 schools in 5 matchups, 5 winners plus 1 wildcard (best-performing loser) advance
  • Grand Final 2: 6 schools in 3 matchups, 3 winners advance to final faceoff
  • Champion: 3-way final faceoff challenge to determine the MSC Competition crown
  • Overall Winner: The school that emerges victorious from the championship round
  • Interactive Features: Hover over match boxes to highlight them in orange
  • Note: Winner advancement and wildcard selection will be updated dynamically as matches conclude
